Why most businesses leak customers

Your customers don't wait. You can't always answer.

A lead messages you on Instagram at 11pm. A WhatsApp follow-up gets missed because you were in a meeting. An email sits unread for two days. None of these feel like a big deal until you add them up over a month.

Most automation tools promise to fix this by doing more: more dashboards, more notifications, more tabs to check. That's not less work. That's just work wearing a disguise.

Sift does the opposite: it does the work, and tells you only what matters.
